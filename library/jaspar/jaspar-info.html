JASPAR is an open-access and widely used database of manually curated, non-redundant transcription factor (TF) binding profiles stored as position frequency matrices (PFM) and transcription factor flexible models (TFFM) for TFs from species in six taxonomic groups.

Source: Extract from JASPAR about page [2019/10/30]

This plugin is developed by Aziz Khan from JASPAR consortium.
