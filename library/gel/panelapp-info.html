PanelApp is a knowledgebase of virtual gene panels for rare diseases and cancer. It has a crowdsourcing tool that allows experts from around the world to provide reviews of genes and the underlying evidence for disease causation.

Source: Adapted from page `PanelApp News` [2019/05/26]

This plugin was not developed by the Genomics England PanelApp team. Genomics England neither supports nor endorses this work. References to Genomics England and PanelApp in this plugin are meant only to credit the source of data.
